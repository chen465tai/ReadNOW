Service

OpenNow is a local-first reader for opening and reading received files, including Markdown, HTML, web reports, ZIP web packages, PDFs, images, text, Office/RTF files where supported, and other supported file types.

OpenNow is not a legal archive, backup service, or the only safe copy of important files. Keep original files in their source apps or storage locations when they matter.

Free Features

The free version lets you open and read local files without creating an OpenNow account.

OpenNow Pro

OpenNow Pro unlocks paid capabilities available in the app version you use, such as:

  • AI document summary;
  • AI document Q&A;
  • cross-file content search;
  • iCloud sync;
  • PDF export;
  • long-image export;
  • original audio, video, and image download.

Some Pro features depend on system availability, file type support, App Store purchase status, iCloud Drive availability, network availability, or the AI provider you configure.

Subscriptions And Purchases

Monthly and yearly Pro plans are auto-renewable subscriptions purchased through the App Store. They renew automatically unless you cancel in App Store subscription settings before renewal.

Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks long-term Pro access for the Apple ID that purchased it.

Apple handles billing, renewal, cancellation, refund requests, family sharing availability, and subscription management. OpenNow provides in-app access to Apple subscription management when available.

AI Provider Keys

Online AI uses your own provider key. You are responsible for your AI provider account, usage, billing, rate limits, provider availability, and compliance with the provider's terms.

OpenNow stores your configured API key in Keychain on your device. Do not enter keys that you are not authorized to use.

User Content

You are responsible for the files and prompts you choose to open, import, sync, export, share, download, or send to an AI provider.

Do not use OpenNow to process content that you do not have the right to use, or content that violates applicable law.

Availability And Changes

OpenNow may change, add, or remove features over time. Pro access covers the paid capabilities available in the app version you use; newly added capabilities may require a compatible app version, system version, supported file type, or active Pro entitlement.

服务说明

即刻阅览是一款本地优先的文件阅读器，用于打开和阅读收到的文件，包括 Markdown、HTML、网页报告、ZIP 网页包、PDF、图片、文本、可支持的 Office/RTF 文件和其他受支持的文件类型。

即刻阅览不是法律归档工具、备份服务，也不应作为重要文件的唯一副本。重要原文件仍建议保留在来源应用或原始存储位置。

免费功能

免费版允许你在无需创建即刻阅览账号的情况下打开和阅读本地文件。

即刻阅览 Pro

即刻阅览 Pro 解锁你所使用 App 版本中已经提供的付费能力，例如：

  • AI 文档总结；
  • AI 文档问答；

  • iCloud 同步；
  • PDF 导出；
  • 长图导出；
  • 音频、视频和图片原文件下载。

部分 Pro 功能取决于系统能力、文件类型支持、App Store 购买状态、iCloud Drive 可用性、网络可用性，或你配置的 AI 服务商。
